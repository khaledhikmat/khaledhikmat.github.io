About Me

I am a software technologist and architect. I work as a chief technology officer for an international company headquartered in the US that deals with hotel management and marketing. I have been with this company for 17 years and I really love my job.

My recent technology interest has been Mobile development, Web APIs and Microsoft Azure. In my long career, I started with Motorola 68000 and Z8000 assembly languages, then C, then C++, then Java, then C#, JavaScript and Node.

In my blog posts, I will touch mainly on three different categories: Technical, Health and Arabic. Although I often write in my company’s internal blogs and keep many notes in my Office OneNote, I don't often write publicly! This blog site is my first attempt to change that and try to merge with the rest of the world by sharing some of my personal and technical experiences.
